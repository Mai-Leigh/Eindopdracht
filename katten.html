Adopt don't shop

Adopting a cat instead of buying one had multiple advantages. U are giving them a second chance at life once u adopt them. It also reduces the number of stray cats, because at the moment there are between approximately 135.590 and 1.200.000. Most cats at shelters are already vaccinated, had their paws spayed, had their health examined and most of them were also neutered. A lot of them already have such big personalities and that makes every one of them unique. By adopting a cat u support several important causes such as:

  • Overpopulation
  • Unenthical breeder
  • Responsible pet ownership

Overpopulation:
By making sure that adopted cats are spayed or neutered we can prevent unwanted litters. As said before in the intro there are a lot of stray cays. it could be that this was a result of cats not being neutered and causing litters.
