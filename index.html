"A safe place for cats"

why the poezenboot?

“The Poezenboot, the world’s only floating cat sanctuary, is a peaceful safe harbor on the Amsterdam’s canals. Since the 1960s, this unique shelter has lovingly rescued stray and abandoned cats, giving each one a safe, warm place to heal and thrive aboard a cozy boat. Run entirely through the dedication of volunteers and the generosity of donors, the Poezenboot has become a cherished symbol of compassion and care. Visitors can feel the warmth and calm as they step aboard, knowing they’re supporting a sanctuary built on love and community.”
